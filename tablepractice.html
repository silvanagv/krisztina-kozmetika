Directions

Direction to the salon from HWY 40 (from Hillsborough): Take exit 266 and take a left when you get off the exit on to Martin Luther King Junior Road. Go straight until the intersection of Estes Drive and Martin Luther King Jr. Take a right at the intersection (you will pass a railroad crossing) and go straight until the road ends and the only choice you will have is left or right - take a left on to North Greensboro Street. Go straight until the intersection of Weaver Street and North Greensboro Road, then take a right at the intersection. We are two doors down from PNC Bank.

If you come from Durham or Raleigh, and you come from HWY 40 then take exit 270 ,take a left at the exit onto 15 501 South and go straight until the Estes Drive intersection. Take a right at the intersection and go straight until the road ends and only choice you will have is left or right.Take the left on to North Greensboro Street until the Weaver Street Intersection,and take a right there onto Weaver Street. We are two doors down from PNC Bank on the right. It's a big blue building.

From HWY 64, or Farrington Village or Governors' Club take 15 501 North until you see the sign for HWY 54 West.Take a left onto 54 West towards Carrboro, and come off at the exit South Greensboro Street and take a left at the exit for a couple hundred yards and then take a right onto Greensboro Street. Go straight until you pass Open Eye Cafe on your right and at the intersection of Greensboro and Weaver Street take a left. The Salon is on the right side where the PNC bank is. It's a blue building. We are just two doors down from the bank.
